<objective>
Add a new `draw_battery_indicator()` function to `app.py` that draws a classic battery icon
(outline body + right-side nub + state-dependent fill) onto the display image, mirroring the
rotation-aware viewer-space technique of `draw_date_overlay()`. The icon is warning-only: it
renders a partial fill for a low battery, an empty outline for a flat battery, and nothing at
all for a healthy battery. Two hardcoded threshold constants drive the three states.

This plan is the TDD core of the phase — it produces the pure drawing logic and its contract
tests. Plan 13-02 wires it into config, the render pipeline, and the settings UI.

Purpose: Give the user an at-a-glance low-battery warning on the e-paper photo itself.
Output: `draw_battery_indicator()` + `BATTERY_LOW_THRESHOLD`/`BATTERY_FLAT_THRESHOLD` in app.py,
and `tests/test_battery_indicator.py` exercising the three states, the no-op path, and the
position lookup.
</objective>
